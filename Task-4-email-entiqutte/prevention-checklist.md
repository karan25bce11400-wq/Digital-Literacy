✅ Do's

Do think before posting and consider the impact on your reputation.

Do maintain a professional and respectful tone in all posts and comments.

Do use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

Do verify sources before sharing information to avoid spreading false news.

Do engage positively with peers, professors, and professional networks.

❌ Don'ts

Don't share personal information such as your address, phone number, or passwords.

Don't post offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.

Don't accept friend requests or connections from unknown individuals without caution.

Don't overshare or post private details about yourself or others.

Don't spend excessive time online at the cost of your academics or mental health.
