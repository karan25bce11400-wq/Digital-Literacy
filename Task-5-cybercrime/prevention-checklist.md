
# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist for College Students

1. **Use strong, unique passwords** for each account and change them regularly.
2. **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** on email, social media, and banking accounts.
3. **Verify sender details** before clicking on links or downloading attachments from emails or messages.
4. **Keep your devices updated** with the latest software, antivirus, and security patches.
5. **Avoid sharing UPI PINs, OTPs, or banking credentials** with anyone, even if they claim to be officials.
6. **Use official banking apps and websites** for transactions; avoid third-party links for payments.
7. **Be cautious on public Wi-Fi**; avoid accessing sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.
8. **Limit personal information shared online** and review privacy settings on social media platforms.
9. **Educate yourself about phishing and online scams**; do not respond to suspicious messages demanding money.
10. **Report cybercrimes immediately** via the [National Cyber Crime Portal](https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline at **1930**.
